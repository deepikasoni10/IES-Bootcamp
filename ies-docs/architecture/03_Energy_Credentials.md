# Energy Credentials

## Overview

Digital attestations tied to Energy Resource Addresses that provide verifiable claims about resources - such as green energy certification, ownership status, maintenance logs, subsidy eligibility, and transaction history.

## Key Points

- **Standard**: Can be based on W3C Verifiable Credentials Data Model
- **Components**: Issuer (certification body, utility, government), Holder (resource owner), Verifier (marketplace, app)
- **Properties**: Cryptographically secure, privacy-preserving (selective disclosure), machine-verifiable, decentralized, revocable
