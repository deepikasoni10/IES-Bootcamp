## Intra-Discom P2P Trading

For P2P energy trading between prosumers within the same discom, see the [IES P2P Trading Specification](https://github.com/India-Energy-Stack/ies-specs/tree/main/p2p-trading). 
This specification covers the simpler case where both buyer and seller are under the same distribution company, eliminating the need for inter-discom coordination, cross-utility enforcement, and inter-retailer settlement mechanisms.
